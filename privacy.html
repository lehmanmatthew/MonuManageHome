Who we are

MonuManage ("MonuManage," "we," "us") provides order-tracking and proof-approval software for monument and memorial companies. This policy covers monumanage.com and its subpages, and, at a summary level, the MonuManage application itself. It applies to visitors to this site, people who request a demo, and — where noted — the families who use a shared proof link sent by one of our customers.

Data controller / entity: [Legal company name], [registered address].

Information we collect

Information you give us directly

  • Demo requests. When you submit the form on our demo request page, we collect your name, company name, email, and the role, location count, current software, and message fields you fill in. Phone number is optional.
  • Correspondence. If you email us, we keep that conversation and any details you choose to share in it.

Information collected automatically

  • Theme preference. If you accept our cookie banner, we store your light/dark mode choice in your browser's local storage. This is not sent to us or to any third party — it only persists on your device.
  • Basic web server logs. Our hosting provider may log standard technical data (IP address, browser type, pages requested) for security and reliability purposes, in line with its own privacy practices.

We do not use advertising cookies or trackers, and we do not sell personal information.

How we use information

  • To respond to demo requests and schedule calls.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve this website and our product.
  • To send you information you've asked for about MonuManage.
  • To meet legal, security, and contractual obligations.

Where GDPR applies, our legal bases are: your consent (for example, ticking the consent box on the demo form), performance of a contract or steps toward one, and our legitimate interest in operating and marketing this service, balanced against your rights.

Cookies & local storage

The only browser storage this site uses is a single local-storage key that remembers your light/dark theme choice, set only after you accept the cookie banner. Declining still lets the toggle work for that visit; it just won't persist. We don't use analytics or advertising cookies on this marketing site as of this policy's last-updated date above — if that changes, we'll update this section and, where required, ask for renewed consent.

How we share information

We share demo-request information with:

  • Formspree, the form-processing service that delivers demo request submissions to our team. See Formspree's own privacy policy for how it handles form data in transit.
  • Google Fonts, used to load the typefaces on this page. Font files are served by Google's infrastructure when the page loads.
  • Service providers who host, secure, or support our infrastructure, bound by contract to protect your information.
  • Authorities, where required by law, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice where required.

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

Data retention

We keep demo request information for as long as reasonably needed to respond to your inquiry and, if you become a customer, for the duration of that relationship plus a standard retention period afterward for legal and accounting purposes. You can ask us to delete it sooner — see "Your rights" below.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export the personal information we hold about you, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent at any time. This includes rights under the EU/UK GDPR for individuals in the EEA and UK, and under the CCPA/CPRA and similar state laws for California and other U.S. residents.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. We won't discriminate against you for making a request. If you're in the EEA or UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures — including encryption in transit and, within the product itself, at rest — to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

This site and our product are intended for business use by monument and memorial companies and their adult customers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and this service is not directed at anyone under 16.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll change the "Last updated" date above, and if a change is significant, we'll take reasonable steps to let you know.
