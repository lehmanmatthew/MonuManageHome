Family portal

Let families approve a design without a phone call.

Send a secure link. No account, no password required on their end. The family opens it, sees the current proof and the order status, and clicks Approve or Request Changes.

Approval carries a timestamp and locks that exact proof version, so there's a record if the question ever comes up again. A revision request opens a comment thread your staff can see directly on the order, instead of a note scribbled after a call.
